Pasta

Description

Agilo e oilo pasta is a classic, traditional Italian pasta dish that literally translates to "garlic and oil"

Ingredients

  • 1 lb(≈450g) spaghetti
  • 1 tsp(≈6g) of salt
  • 1 bulb garlic (about 20 cloves, peeled and sliced thin)
  • ¼ tsp(≈0.6g) of freshly cracked black pepper
  • ½ tsp(≈0.55g) of cracked red pepper
  • ½ cup(≈118ml) of olive oil
  • ½ cup(≈55g) of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp(≈4g) Italian parsley (minced)
  • 2 tbsp(≈30ml) lemon juice
  • ¼ cup(≈60ml) pasta water (reserved from cooking the spaghetti)
  • hi

Recipe

  1. Gather ingredients and set a large pot of water to boil, adding ½ tsp salt to the water.
  2. Once pasta has been cooked until al dente, about 10 minutes, strain the pasta, reserving ¼ cup of pasta water.
  3. In a large saucepan over medium-low heat, sauté sliced garlic with black pepper, ½ tsp salt, and red pepper flakes in olive oil. Sauté until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
  4. Pour strained cooked pasta into the large saucepan with the sautéed garlic and oil.
  5. Toss thoroughly so every piece of spaghetti is coated in garlicky oil. Then, add grated parmesan cheese, parsley, lemon juice, and 2 Tbsp of pasta water. Toss to combine so the cheese melts into the sauce and covers all of the pasta. Add a little more starchy pasta water, only if needed to achieve a looser texture if desired.
